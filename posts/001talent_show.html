Put Together an Act

Music, skits, art, you name it! By yourself or with a group, come up with an act to perform for the YSA. No auditions required! We're very excited to see what you have to offer us.

Purpose of the Show

Our heavenly father has given us many talents that we're blessed to use in our daily lives. It's important for us to not only thank the Lord for the amazing talents we've been given but also to use them when needed. This talent show will act as a showcase for some of the wonderful gifts we've been blessed with.
