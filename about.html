We are the Spokane River YSA Ward!

Being formed out of combining the Shawnee YSA and the Mount Spokane YSA branch, the Spokane River YSA ward is one of two YSA wards in the Spokane area, meant to keep us connected spiritually and socially. Our location, 732 West Indiana Avenue, also known as The Gathering Place, has been allocated to us to come together.

What do we do?

Serve

The Lord has commanded us to serve one another and grow closer to Christ. Service is something the savior taught and is something we need to make as a routine in our life. Our goal is to serve as much as possible. Join us for service projects, big and small. Whether it's fixing a friend's ceiling, or handing out meals to the homeless, we always strive to serve.

We are all called to serve.

Connect

Our community is centered around Christ. This means we are all there for each other and do all we can to connect and come together. Activities, food, and parties are a regular part of what we do. It's important for us to come together as one.

Join us for fun adventures, a caring community, and people who want to grow closer to Christ together.
